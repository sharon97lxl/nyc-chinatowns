<h1>Map of NYC's Chinatowns</h1>  

This map provides an overview of the three big Chinatowns in NYC (Manhattan, Flushing and Sunset Park), with some basic information on each area. As definitions of 'Chinatowns' aren't generally codified at a census level (other than occasionally the Manhattan one), please take all statistics and boundarise with a grain of salt. 

Note the following about the data used:

**Boundaries:** Taken from a mix of sources. The Manhattan Chinatown boundaries were taken roughly from the NYT Neighborhood Map; the Queens Chinatown boundaries were taken from nycneighborhoods.app/map, and the Brooklyn Chinatown is based on commercial centre of the area (8th Avenue, from 40th - 60th St - which I grabbed from a Google search)

**Population data:** Taken from popfactfinder.planning.nyc.gov. As the areas I've used in my map don't map neatly onto the geographies in the DCP map, I've taken the statistics from the Neighborhood Tabulation ARea (NTA) that each Chinatown primarily belongs to.

**Languages spoken:** Taken from languagemap.nyc. Honestly I initially set out to create a map that could show the linguistic diversity among Chinese communities in NYC, but to my massive disappointment there's no good data on speakers of Chinese topolects in the US (the census just collects data on 'Chinese' (including Mandarin and Cantonese) and 'Min Nan', which I assume is so Taiwanese speakers don't have to say they speak Chinese on the census). The Language Map uses data from the Endangered Language Alliance. I have issues with the way they classified some of the Chinese languages so I tried to use terms that are more recognisable to linguistics (e.g. Min Nan and Min Dong instead of 'Fujianese'
